## Learning objectives

- Know about the `|>` pipe operator for chaining function calls.
- Know about ranges.
  - They are structs.
  - They are enumerable.
  - How to transform them to a list.
- Know about streams.
  - `Enum` functions are eager, `Stream` functions are lazy.
  - Potentially infinite.
  - When to use streams instead of `Enum`.
