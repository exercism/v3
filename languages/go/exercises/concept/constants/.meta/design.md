## Goal

The goal of this exercise is to teach the student about constants in Go.

## Learning objectives

- Understand the purpose of `const`.
- Know when to define `const` values.
- Know how to define `const` values.
- Know how many types of `const` exist.

## Out of scope

## Concepts

- `const`
- `iota`

## Prerequisites

- `basics`
- `comments`
